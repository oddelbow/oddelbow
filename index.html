If you're reading this, I probably told you to check this out. If you stumbled here on your own, I'm so sorry in advance. None of this will be worth your time.

I've been creating art for over a decade, and I wanted a place to compile everything. Here it is.

When I first started making art, I was limited to shitty songs on the ukulele. I could barely sing, and any more than 4 strings seemed like a death sentence. I slowly taught myself to sing without sounding like a horse that had been punched in the throat. Day by day, I learned how to force my cumbersome fingers into coherent shapes on a guitar neck. I wrote and I wrote and I wrote. I practiced, as Kurt Vonnegut would say, the arrangement of twenty-six phonetic symbols, ten numerals, and about eight punctuation marks. This yielded songs, poems, and more recently, a couple novels.
